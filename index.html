Four projects, one philosophy. Decentralized healthcare, bio-collaboration, algorithmic cultural inclusion, fashion-tech convergence — they are not isolated innovations, but projections of IDE's design philosophy across different dimensions. When designers shift from "solving problems" to "defining ecosystems", when technology shifts from "seeking attention" to "gentle restraint", design is no longer the packaging of engineering, nor engineering a tool of design — they are two sides of the same evolution.
